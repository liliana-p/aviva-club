Links

Warning: All links open in new window. To open in same window, click and drag link to search bar.

-✶Wikipedia's Brief Dollz Explanation✶-

In case you're confused about dollz and need a quickie expo on what they are an how they work. Just click the link and it should scroll you down to the only section you need to read. It's just one paragraph, so it should be good enough!


~✶The Doll Palace Web Archive✶~

This is the web archive of the original Doll Palace site. You can find plenty of archived dollz and bases there, as well as leftover articles, stories, etc. Anything requiring php or javascript is mainly busted now, but it's worth a look around. Of course, most of the dollz and bases you can find there, you can find here, just without the crappy load time.


-✶Life At The Palace✶-

A study on the Palace chat and all the intricate little details one must know to interact there. This can be simple guide to refer to in case of any dollz related confusion found here, or anywhere else.


~✶History Of The Sk8r✶~

This is a history of the sk8r doll and dollz in general. An imperative know if you're serious about dollz (Especially on the Palace). It's also just an interesting piece of internet history if you're into noughties interweb trends.


-✶An Elegy for a Palace✶-

Essay about The Doll Palace as a whole and the culture behind the entire community. The author goes well in depth and the story is a good read! Very funny, entertaining, and informative, especially for dollz enthusiasts looking for info on an age long gone.


~✶Dollz Netiquette✶~

A code of conduct for using and distributing dollz on the internet. If you plan on doing anything dollz related, even just browsing dollz sites, this is a good read. It'll give you all the info you need to know about the unsaid rules of dolling. Even if you don't engage in dolling, this can help you understand what is and isn't allowed so you can call people out when they're being disrespectful!


~✶Palace Guide✶~

A basic guide to using the Palace. If you're having trouble understanding how to do something, or don't understand why something was done, check this guide for info. Something to note is that you don't need to use multiple props to get a big prop anymore. That doesn't mean old avatars aren't still segmented into 44x44 props.
